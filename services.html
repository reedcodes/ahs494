I am currently available for hire on web-based projects that require accessibility production, and I offer a variety of training and consulting services for accessible web development.

Services

  • responsive web design
  • accessibility training with HTML and ARIA
  • understanding and applying WCAG and Section 508
  • online content development
  • policy development for web, including video and audio

Rates

Service Fees Description
training sessions $600 per 3-hour session Training topics vary from Thinking Responsively to Handcrafted HTML with ARIA. Discounts available for full-day or full-week curricula.
content and code consultation $100 per hour, minimum 3 hours Consultation items encompass everything from HTML5 compliance to responsive design reviews to updating accessible audio and video.
accessible and responsive web development $75 per hour, minimum 6 hours This includes full site development from start to finish, from existing designs or brand materials.
website accessibility audit $65 per hour, minimum 2 hours Audits cover compliance with WCAG 2.0 AA and Section 508. Additional fees may apply for WCAG 2.0 AAA compliance. Does not include fixing errors, warnings, or issues (see above for web development).
policy development fees vary, starting at $50 per hour Fees for policy work depend on the level of detail needed for the organization or team.
