Everyone, regardless of ability, deserves access to modern technologies.

As more companies and organizations place greater importance on full-stack engineering and advanced JavaScript frameworks, an essential need for a skilled front-end specialist emerges. Someone who understands the intricacies of creating website with accessibility in mind brings a wealth of knowledge onto any team and works right alongside front-end, back-end and full-stack developers as well as content strategists, usability designers, project managers, graphic artists, social media marketers, digital strategists, copywriters, and more.

That's where I come in.

My expertise lies with semantic, well-coded, and accessible HTML and CSS. While I am not a full-stack developer, I can and do work with JavaScript and other platforms, but a terrific web app is only as accessible as its front-end, and that's where I direct my attention.
