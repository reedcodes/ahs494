The internet is everywhere, and the ability to access information on the web is often taken for granted. The assumption that a website or web app is available to everyone fails to taking into account the needs of those using assistive technology, running older or slower computers, or browsing on satelitte or dial-up speeds.

Everyone, regardless of ability, deserves access to modern technologies.

A terrific web app is only as accessible as its front-end, and that's where I direct my attention. I'm a senior-level front-end developer who understands the intricacies of creating websites with accessibility in mind, and bring a wealth of knowledge regarding usable and accessible front-end code to any team. You can read more about me, including my skills and qualifications, learn about my services and rates, or contact me for a quote or more information.
